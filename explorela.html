Things to do in LA!

  1. Go shopping at Grand Central Market, a European-style food hall located in Downtown LA.
  2. If you want to see a waterfall, take a hike to Eaton Canyon Park.
  3. If you have a dog, go to the Leo Carrillo dog-friendly beach in Malibu, or the Huntington Dog Beach.
  4. On the exterior of the U.S. Bank building in LA, there is a glass slide 1,000 feet high that is open to the public.
  5. Go to the Griffith Observatory at night to see a great view of lit up LA.
  6. If you're looking for an easy hike with a view, the Hollywood sign hike would be perfect for you.
