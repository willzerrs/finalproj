Health & Beauty tips!

  1. Stay hydrated. Water helps keep your energy levels up, clear skin, boosts metabolism, and more. Drink plenty of water each day!
  2. Get at least 30 minutes of activity every day! Working out regularly improves brain power, boosts your immune system, and prevents breakdowns.
  3. Stay off electronics at a minimum of 1 hour before you plan on going to sleep in order to improve your sleep quality.
  4. Use sunscreen with an SPF of 30 at lowest everyday, even when you can't see the sun.
  5. In order to cut out junk food, fill up on fruits and veggies first. More often than not, your cravings will be satisfied.
  6. Eat a big breakfast, medium lunch, and a light dinner, so that you work off the calories throughout the day.
