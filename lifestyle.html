Lifestyle & Mental Health

  1. Prepare meals on the weekends so that you can save yourself time through the week.
  2. Work on your relationships and friendships. Having strong social support prevents depression and has been linked to warding off diseases.
  3. Learn how to meditate and do it regularly! Meditation is good for mental and physical health and can help treat anxiety-related disorders.
  4. If you don't already have a "flow activity," find one and participate in it regularly.
  5. Writing in a journal has been proven to help people relieve stress and to let out pent-up emotions.
  6. Many people swear by writing down things you are grateful for everyday to make yourself feel better and more grounded.
